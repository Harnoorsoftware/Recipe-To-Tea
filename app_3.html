Recipe to tea(Well, I had enough of story blogs so I decided to start a cooking blog!!!)

Materials : a utensil for making tea, 2 cups water and 1 milk, tea leaves and sugar ,ginger, cardamom , cloves, star anise, black pepper, cinnamon (measurements as per your taste), a tea strainer and cup .
Steps Images
Pour 2 cups of water into the utensil. img_water.png
Add tea leaves, sugar, ginger, cloves, cinnnamon, star anise, black pepper and cardamom and let it boil. img_ingredient.png
After boiling it ,add milk to it and let it boil thoroughly. img_boiling_tea.png
Lastly, stain it in a cup and enjoy! img_tea_cup.png
